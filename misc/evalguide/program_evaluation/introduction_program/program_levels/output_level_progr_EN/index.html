Results level

The products of the program or of its individual projects represent the focus for this level. Evaluation questions for this level arise towards the end of the implementation phase.

Which products have been created?

An overview of the developed products and of their usage shows the specific output of the program or its individual projects. These products can be learning media or infrastructure, or even whole courses.


How are the products to be evaluated?

The products developed can be evaluated according to generic assessment criteria using either data from the evaluation of individual projects or gathering new data (e.g. usingcriteria catalogues) if  the data does not exist or is not available in a sufficiently structured form.
